Shipping FAQ

What is the delivery time for standard shipping?
Standard shipping typically takes 5 to 7 business days from the date your order is processed and shipped. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping carrier’s service schedule.

How fast is expedited shipping?
Expedited shipping is available for customers who need faster delivery. This option usually ensures your package arrives within 2 to 3 business days after the order has been processed. Additional charges may apply.

Will I receive tracking information?
Yes, once your order has been shipped, a shipping confirmation email will be sent to you. This email will include a tracking number and a direct link to track your shipment through the carrier’s website. Please allow up to 24 hours for the tracking information to become active.
