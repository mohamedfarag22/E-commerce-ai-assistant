Return Policy

Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a product that is damaged or defective, you are eligible to return it within 30 days of delivery. Upon inspection, you may choose to receive either a full refund or a replacement item at no additional cost.

Return Conditions

Items must be returned in their original packaging and include all accessories, manuals, and any promotional materials (if applicable).

A proof of purchase—such as an order confirmation email or receipt—is required to process all returns.

Return Process
To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team with your order number and a description of the issue. We will provide instructions and a return shipping label if the return is approved.
